In this short paragraph, I will explore in brief the origin of this product.

Chocolate is a food made from roasted and ground coca beans that can be a:

  • Liquid,
  • Solid,
  • Paste, with different flavours like fruits.

The history of chocolate dates back more than 5000 years, when the coca tree was first domesticated in present-day southeast Ecuador.
Then it was introduced to Mesoamerica, where coca drinks gained significance as an elite beverage among cultures like the Maya.

Coca was considered as a:

  1. Gift
  2. Medicine
  3. Ceremonies.

In modern life, Chocolate becomes one of the most ingredients used to make desserts, ice creams, cakes, mousse, and cookies.
